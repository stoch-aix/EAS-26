1. Probability Density Function (PDF) of Latency [ms]

Reading: Probability is the area under the curve.

$$f(x) = \frac{1}{x\sigma\sqrt{2\pi}} \exp\left( -\frac{(\ln x - \mu)^2}{2\sigma^2} \right)$$

2. Cumulative Distribution Function (CDF)

Reading: Probability is the vertical distance $\Delta y$.

$$F(x) = \Phi\left( \frac{\ln x - \mu}{\sigma} \right)$$

3. Quantile Function (Inverse CDF)

Reading: Probability is represented on the x-axis ($p$), mapping to values on the y-axis.

$$Q(p) = \exp(\mu + \sigma \Phi^{-1}(p))$$
